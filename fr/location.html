We are so excited to celebrate our wedding with you in Arêches! We understand getting to France is a big adventure for everyone so here are some links to help you organize your trip.

Getting to France from the U.S.

You can fly into either Paris Roissy Charles de Gaulles (CDG) or Geneva International Airport (GVA).

From Paris

If you fly into Paris, you can take a train to Albertville. You can reserve train tickets here http://www.voyages-sncf.com/. From Albertville, there are shuttles to the town of Arêches, here is a link to the shuttle company, you do not need to reserve this shuttle in advance. Or you can rent a car near the train station in Albertville.

Click this link for driving directions from the Albertville train station to Arêches

If you decide to rent a car in Paris, the town of Arêches is a 6 hour drive, click this link for driving directions.

From Geneva

If you fly into Geneva you will need to rent a car. The town of Arêches is an hour and forty minute drive from Geneva, click this link for driving directions.

We will also arrange a bus leaving from the Geneva Airport on Thursday, June 19th and returning to the Geneva Airport on Monday, June 23rd. More details to come as the date approaches.
