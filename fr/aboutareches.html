Weather

Areches is an incredibly beautiful area in the French alps and is about 1500 feet in elevation, the ceremony and reception sites are around 3,000 feet and both are outdoor locations. We are hoping that the weather will be beautiful in the end of June, but temperature can range from eighty to forty degrees fahrenheit in the summer so we recommend that you bring some warm clothing and sunscreen. Hiking and other outdoor activities

Hiking is our favorite way to discover the magic of the Beaufortain!
Click this link to learn more.

We love hiking along the beaufortain trail and to Lac des Tempetes.

If you go to the Office of Tourism in Areches, they can give you more detailed information.

There are also many shops in the town of Areches that rent bikes, climbing gear, etc and can set you up with memorable adventures.

CHEESE!!!!

The town of Beaufort is world famous for its cheese (Thibaut calls it "The King of the Cheese"), the milk is protected by the Government, effectively sustaining the rural traditional lifestyle of the farmers of the area, the heritage livestock and the pristine landscapes.

We highly recomend stopping by the Beaufort Cheese cooperative, http://www.cooperative-de-beaufort.com/
